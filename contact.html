Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduSchedule free to use?

Yes! EduSchedule is completely free to use. All features including timetable generation, conflict detection, and export options are available at no cost.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required! Your timetable data is stored locally in your browser, ensuring privacy and instant access without any registration process.

Can I use EduSchedule on mobile devices?

Absolutely! EduSchedule is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

How do I export my timetable?

You can export your timetable as a CSV file or print it directly from the generator page. Just click the export or print buttons in the controls section.

What if I encounter a bug or issue?

Please report any bugs using the contact form above or email us directly at support@eduschedule.com. We take all reports seriously and fix issues quickly.

Can I suggest new features?

We love feature suggestions! Use the contact form with "Feature Request" as the subject, or reach out to us through any of our communication channels.
