Our Mission

At EduSchedule, we believe that every student deserves a stress-free way to manage their academic schedule. Our mission is to provide the most intuitive, powerful, and accessible timetable generator that helps students focus on what matters most - their education.

We understand the challenges students face when trying to organize their classes, avoid conflicts, and maintain a balanced schedule. That's why we've created a solution that's not just functional, but delightful to use.

Our Story

EduSchedule was born out of frustration. As computer science students, we spent countless hours manually creating timetables, dealing with conflicts, and constantly reorganizing our schedules. We knew there had to be a better way.

What started as a simple project to solve our own problem has grown into a comprehensive platform used by thousands of students worldwide. We've listened to feedback, iterated on our design, and continuously improved the experience.

Today, EduSchedule represents our commitment to making education more accessible and organized for everyone.

Our Values

Student-Centric
